Pilot Program Terms

Terms and conditions governing participation in the OpsPal Pilot Program.

OpsPal Pilot Program Terms These terms apply to all participants in the OpsPal Pilot Program and are supplemental to your existing RevPal Master Agreement.

Purpose

The OpsPal Pilot Program (“Pilot”) provides selected RevPal customers early access to OpsPal, an AI-driven agent system developed by RevPal.

The purpose of the Pilot is for target personas to test and provide feedback on OpsPal’s functionality, enabling RevPal to refine the product prior to broader commercial release.

OpsPal is an experimental AI service and is provided for testing and evaluation purposes only.

Eligibility & Termination

Participation is limited to active RevPal customers in good standing under an existing services agreement (“Master Agreement”).

Access to OpsPal is contingent upon:

  • An active RevPal agreement
  • Current payment status
  • Acceptance of these Pilot Terms

If a participant’s RevPal agreement terminates for any reason, access to OpsPal will terminate concurrently. RevPal may suspend or terminate Pilot access at its discretion.

Experimental AI Disclosure

Participant acknowledges and agrees:

  • OpsPal is an experimental artificial intelligence system under active development.
  • Outputs may contain errors, omissions, inconsistencies, or unintended results.
  • System logic, routing, workflows, and behaviors may change without notice.
  • Performance and reliability are being evaluated and are not guaranteed.

RevPal does not warrant:

  • Accuracy of AI-generated outputs
  • Continuous availability
  • Error-free operation
  • Fitness for any specific operational purpose

Participants are solely responsible for reviewing, validating, and approving all AI-generated outputs before operational reliance. OpsPal shall not be used as the sole decision-making authority for mission-critical production systems during the Pilot.

Dedicated Slack Channel & Support

During the Pilot, each participating organization will be provided a dedicated Slack channel for direct support and communication with the RevPal team. The dedicated Slack channel serves multiple purposes:

  • Real-time support from RevPal as it pertains to OpsPal
  • Issue reporting and troubleshooting
  • Collection of performance insights and improvement data

Participants acknowledge and agree that:

  • RevPal may monitor activity within the dedicated Slack channel for purposes of support, troubleshooting, and analytics.
  • Feedback, usage observations, and interaction metadata shared in the channel may be reviewed by RevPal personnel as part of Pilot oversight.
  • Participants are responsible for ensuring that any data shared within the Slack channel complies with their internal data governance policies.

Scope of Pilot Access

During the Pilot, participants may test capabilities including:

  • AI-driven agent workflows
  • Agent routing and coordination
  • Reliability and stability testing
  • Response formatting and preference configuration
  • Operational adjustments and rule modifications

RevPal reserves the right to modify, limit, suspend, or remove functionality at any time.

Data Protection & Sandbox Testing

RevPal takes reasonable and commercially appropriate steps to protect customer data and maintain system integrity. However, due to the experimental nature of OpsPal, risks remain inherent in AI-driven systems.

Participants acknowledge and agree:

  • Testing in a sandbox environment is strongly recommended.
    • Full Copy Sandbox is preferred.
    • Partial Copy Sandbox is acceptable.
  • Production deployment is undertaken at Participant’s own risk.

RevPal is not liable for:

  • Accidental data loss
  • Data corruption
  • Misrouted updates
  • Configuration changes triggered by AI actions
  • System or workflow interruptions

Participants are responsible for maintaining appropriate backup procedures, validation workflows, and change controls.

Feedback & Improvement

Participants agree to provide:

Passive Feedback

  • Usage data
  • System logs
  • Performance telemetry

Structured Feedback

  • Bi-weekly feedback sessions
  • Input on routing logic, reliability, usability, and actionability

Participant grants RevPal a perpetual, royalty-free right to use anonymized feedback and insights for product improvement and commercialization.

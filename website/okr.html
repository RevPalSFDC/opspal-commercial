Why it's different

Most OKR tools are glorified spreadsheets. OpsPal OKR is a data engine that grounds every target in evidence and improves with each cycle.

Live data baselines

SOQL queries and HubSpot API calls pull real pipeline, conversion, and revenue metrics — no manual entry.

Three-stance targets

Aggressive, Base, and Conservative targets with P10/P50/P90 confidence bands for honest planning.

Bayesian learning engine

Records outcomes and calibrates future targets using Beta priors. Requires 4+ cycles before decision-grade — transparent about its own confidence.

5-dimension initiative scoring

100-point scale with stage modifiers, GTM bonuses, and funnel leverage. Cuts through opinion-based prioritization.

Cascade integrity auditing

100-point alignment score across objectives. Detects orphan KRs, misaligned initiatives, and coverage gaps.

Human-in-the-loop approval

Nothing activates without explicit review. Drafts, scores, and reports are proposals — humans decide.

How OKR cycles work

Five steps from live data to approved OKRs to learning.

1
Snapshot — Pull live revenue, pipeline, and funnel data from connected platforms.
2
Generate — Draft OKRs with three-stance targets and confidence bands from the snapshot.
3
Prioritize — Score and rank initiatives with 5-dimension rubric and capacity-aware cut lines.
4
Approve — Human-in-the-loop review and sign-off. Optionally sync to Asana for execution tracking.
5
Retrospective — Record outcomes, feed the learning engine, and calibrate future targets.

Common Questions

Why is OpsPal Core required?

Core provides the orchestration engine, agent routing, plugin management, and reporting infrastructure that every domain module depends on. It's the foundation — without it, the platform modules can't function.

Can I start with one domain and add more later?

Yes. Most customers start with their primary platform (usually Salesforce or HubSpot) and add modules as their team's usage expands. There's no migration or re-installation required — just add the plugin.

What do I need to get started?

OpsPal runs as a plugin suite inside Claude Code or Codex CLI. You'll need an active subscription to one of these platforms. Our primary development focus has been on Claude Code, though many OpsPal plugin assets are also compatible with Codex CLI.

What does it cost to run OpsPal day-to-day?

In addition to your OpsPal subscription, you'll need a Claude Code or Codex CLI plan from their respective providers. OpsPal is the tooling layer — the underlying AI platform is a separate subscription.

Can I purchase Claude Code or Codex licensing through RevPal?

No. Claude Code and Codex CLI are licensed directly through Anthropic and OpenAI respectively. RevPal does not resell or bundle third-party AI platform subscriptions.

How is my data managed and handled?

Your data stays local to the device where the work is performed — OpsPal does not transmit your org data, credentials, or business information to RevPal servers. We do collect anonymized diagnostic data through our session reflection system, which is entirely opt-in. All reflection data is sanitized and obfuscated at the point of generation, before it ever leaves your machine.

# BLUF+4 Executive Summary Template

**Purpose:** Standardized executive summary format for non-technical stakeholders

**Target Length:** 150-220 words total

**Format:** BLUF+4 (Bottom Line Up Front + 4 supporting sections)

---

## Template

```handlebars
# Executive Summary

**Report**: {{reportType}} | **Org**: {{org}} | **Date**: {{date}}

---

## Bottom Line
{{#if severity}}**{{severity}}**: {{/if}}{{headline}}

{{#if recommendation}}**Recommendation**: {{recommendation}}{{/if}}
{{#if roi}}**Projected Impact**: {{roi}}{{/if}}

---

## Situation
{{#if healthScore}}**Health Score**: {{healthScore}}/100 {{healthIcon}}{{/if}}

{{#each keyFindings}}
- {{icon}} {{text}}{{#if metric}} ({{metric}}){{/if}}
{{/each}}

{{#if comparison}}**vs Benchmark**: {{comparison}}{{/if}}

---

## Next Steps
{{#each nextSteps}}
{{@index}}. **{{action}}**{{#if owner}} (@{{owner}}){{/if}}{{#if timeline}} - {{timeline}}{{/if}}
{{/each}}

---

## Risks & Blockers
{{#if risks}}
{{#each risks}}
- {{icon}} **{{type}}**: {{description}}{{#if mitigation}} | *Mitigation*: {{mitigation}}{{/if}}
{{/each}}
{{else}}
No critical risks identified.
{{/if}}

---

## Support Needed
{{#if decisions}}
**Decisions Required**:
{{#each decisions}}
- {{decision}} (by @{{owner}}, {{deadline}})

{{/if}}
```

## Word Count Guidelines

| Section | Purpose | Target | Max |
|---------|---------|--------|-----|
| **Bottom Line** | Conclusion/Recommendation | 25-35 words | 40 |
| **Situation** | Current state assessment | 30-40 words | 50 |
| **Next Steps** | Prioritized actions | 35-45 words | 55 |
| **Risks/Blockers** | Impediments | 25-35 words | 40 |

## Required Elements Checklist

**Must Include:**
- [ ] Bottom line headline (required)
- [ ] At least 1 key finding (required)
- [ ] At least 1 next step (required)
- [ ] Report metadata (type, org, date)

**Recommended:**
- [ ] Health score with icon
- [ ] ROI or projected impact
- [ ] Severity indicator
- [ ] Owner for each action
- [ ] Timeline for each action
- [ ] Mitigation for each risk

**Must Exclude:**
- [ ] Process details ("I ran a query...")
- [ ] Technical jargon (SOQL, APEX, DML)
- [ ] Vague quantifiers (some, several, many)
- [ ] Filler words (basically, actually, really)

## Good Example

```markdown
# Executive Summary

**Report**: CPQ Assessment | **Org**: acme-corp | **Date**: 2025-11-25

---

## Bottom Line
**ACTION REQUIRED**: CPQ pricing accuracy at 67% is impacting quote margins and extending sales cycles by 3.2 days.

**Recommendation**: Prioritize pricing rule cleanup and approval workflow optimization within 30 days.
**Projected Impact**: $127K annual savings, 15% faster quote turnaround

---

## Situation
**Health Score**: 58/100 :yellow_circle: Moderate

- :red_circle: Pricing rules: 45% contain errors (127 of 283)
- :yellow_circle: Approval workflows: 8 bottlenecks causing 72-hour delays
- :green_circle: Product catalog: 95% accurate
- :warning: Discount schedules: 23 expired rules still active

---

## Next Steps
1. **Archive redundant pricing rules** (@sales-ops) - Week 1-2
2. **Consolidate approval workflows from 8 to 3** (@cpq-admin) - Week 2-3
3. **Update expired discount schedules** (@pricing-team) - Week 3

---

## Risks & Blockers
- :red_circle: **CRITICAL**: Approval workflow changes require VP Sales sign-off
- :yellow_circle: **HIGH**: Pricing rule cleanup may temporarily affect active quotes

---

## Support Needed
**Decisions Required**:
- Approve pricing rule archive strategy (by @vp-sales, Dec 6)

**Approvals Needed**:
- Production deployment window (IT Change Board)

**Resources**:
- 2 additional consultant days ($3,200)

---
*Generated by **OpsPal by RevPal** | 2025-11-25*
```

**Word Count**: 178 words (within target)

## Bad Example (What NOT to Do)

```markdown
# Assessment Summary

I ran a comprehensive analysis of your Salesforce CPQ configuration using SOQL
queries and metadata API calls. The script identified several issues with your
pricing rules. Basically, there are some problems that need to be fixed.

## Technical Details
- Executed 47 SOQL queries against QuoteLineItem object
- Found various validation rule errors in SBQQ__Quote__c
- DML operations are hitting governor limits

## What We Did
First, I connected to the org and ran the discovery script...
```

**Problems:**
- Process details instead of outcomes
- Technical jargon (SOQL, DML, governor limits)
- Vague language (several, some, various)
- Filler words (basically)
- Missing BLUF structure
- No actionable recommendations
- No stakeholder-focused content

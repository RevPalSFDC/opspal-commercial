# Remediation Guide

## TOFU Issues

### Low Lead Volume

**Symptoms:**
- Insufficient leads entering funnel
- Pipeline coverage below target
- High dependency on few sources

**Remediation Matrix:**
| Root Cause | Solution | Timeline | Expected Impact |
|------------|----------|----------|-----------------|
| Low traffic | SEO + content investment | 3-6 months | 30-50% increase |
| Poor conversion | Form optimization | 2-4 weeks | 20-40% increase |
| Limited channels | Channel diversification | 1-3 months | 25-50% increase |
| Budget constraints | ABM focused approach | 2-3 months | Quality over quantity |

**Implementation Playbook:**
```
Week 1-2: Audit current sources
- Analyze traffic by channel
- Map conversion rates by source
- Identify highest ROI channels

Week 3-4: Quick wins
- Optimize top forms (reduce fields, improve CTA)
- A/B test landing pages
- Add exit intent popups

Month 2-3: Strategic initiatives
- Launch content marketing program
- Implement SEO improvements
- Expand paid channels
```

| Wrong content | Content alignment | 2-4 weeks | 25-40% quality increase |

## MOFU Issues

### Low MQL to SQL Conversion

**Symptoms:**
- Many MQLs not converting to SQLs
- Long time in MQL stage
- SDR frustration with lead quality

**Remediation Matrix:**
| Root Cause | Solution | Timeline | Expected Impact |
|------------|----------|----------|-----------------|
| Premature handoff | Add nurture stage | 2-3 weeks | 20-35% improvement |
| SDR process issues | Script optimization | 1-2 weeks | 15-25% improvement |
| Misaligned criteria | S&M alignment workshop | 1 week | 25-40% improvement |

**SDR Process Optimization:**
```
Call Structure:
1. Opening (30 sec) - Permission-based opener
2. Discovery (2-3 min) - 3-5 qualifying questions
3. Value Prop (1 min) - Tailored to pain points
4. Meeting Set (1 min) - Strong close
5. Confirmation (30 sec) - Send calendar invite

Key Questions:
- What's driving your interest today?
- What have you tried so far?
- Who else is involved in this decision?
- What's your timeline for making a change?
- What happens if you don't solve this?
```

### Low Meeting Show Rate

**Symptoms:**
- High no-show rate (>20%)
- Last-minute cancellations
- Poor meeting quality

**Remediation Matrix:**
| Root Cause | Solution | Timeline | Expected Impact |
|------------|----------|----------|-----------------|
| No reminders | Automated reminder sequence | 1 week | 15-25% improvement |
| Low commitment | Pre-meeting qualification | 1-2 weeks | 20-30% improvement |
| Wrong timing | Flexible scheduling | Immediate | 10-15% improvement |

**Meeting Reminder Sequence:**
```
T-24h: Email reminder + agenda
T-2h: SMS/calendar reminder
T-15m: Quick confirmation email

Pre-meeting packet:
- Agenda with objectives
- Brief company intro
- Questions to prepare
- Meeting link (prominent)
```

## BOFU Issues

### Low Win Rate

**Symptoms:**
- Opportunities not closing
- High lost rate
- Long sales cycles

**Root Cause Decision Tree:**
```
Is the prospect a good fit?
├── NO → ICP refinement, better qualification
└── YES → Continue

Are we losing to competitors?
├── YES → Competitive positioning, battlecards
└── NO → Continue

Are we losing on price?
├── YES → Value selling training, ROI tools
└── NO → Continue

Are we losing to no decision?
├── YES → Champion building, urgency creation
└── NO → Process/timing issues
```

**Remediation by Lost Reason:**
| Lost Reason | % of Losses | Solution |
|-------------|-------------|----------|
| Price/Budget | 35% | Value selling training, ROI calculator |
| Competitor | 25% | Battlecards, feature comparison sheets |
| No Decision | 20% | Champion identification, business case |
| Timing | 10% | Nurture program, trigger tracking |
| Poor Fit | 10% | Better qualification, ICP updates |

### Long Sales Cycles

**Symptoms:**
- Deals stalling in stages
- Multiple pushbacks
- Forecast unpredictability

**Remediation Matrix:**
| Root Cause | Solution | Timeline | Expected Impact |
|------------|----------|----------|-----------------|
| Complex buying process | Buyer mapping | 2-3 weeks | 15-25% reduction |
| Insufficient urgency | Value quantification | 2-4 weeks | 20-30% reduction |
| Internal bottlenecks | Process optimization | 1-2 weeks | 10-20% reduction |

**Velocity Improvement Tactics:**
```
Discovery Stage:
- Map all stakeholders upfront
- Identify decision process
- Establish timeline with consequences

Proposal Stage:
- Get verbal agreement before sending
- Include expiration date
- Bundle approval steps

Negotiation Stage:
- Pre-negotiate procurement terms
- Have red line items ready
- Maintain executive sponsorship
```

## Cross-Funnel Issues

### Rep Performance Variance

**Symptoms:**
- Wide gap between top and bottom performers
- Inconsistent conversion rates
- Varying activity levels

**Remediation:**
```
1. Document top performer behaviors
   - Call recordings analysis
   - Email template review
   - Pipeline management habits

2. Create playbook from best practices
   - Stage-specific scripts
   - Objection handling guides
   - Time management templates

3. Implement coaching program
   - Weekly 1:1s with data review
   - Call shadowing sessions
   - Role-play exercises

4. Technology enablement
   - Conversation intelligence tools
   - Sales engagement platform
   - Performance dashboards
```

### Pipeline Coverage Issues

**Symptoms:**
- Insufficient pipeline
- Feast/famine cycles
- Inaccurate forecasting

**Target Coverage by Role:**
| Role | Coverage Target | Notes |
|------|-----------------|-------|
| SDR | 150% of SQL target | Account for conversion |
| AE (SMB) | 3x quota | Higher velocity |
| AE (Mid-Market) | 3.5x quota | Moderate velocity |
| AE (Enterprise) | 4x quota | Longer cycles |

**Coverage Remediation:**
```
Immediate (Week 1-2):
- Reactivate stalled opportunities
- Re-engage closed-lost from past 6 months
- Ask for referrals from recent wins

Short-term (Month 1):
- Increase outbound activity
- Launch targeted campaign
- Partner referral push

Medium-term (Quarter):
- Add new lead sources
- Expand target market
- Improve conversion rates
```

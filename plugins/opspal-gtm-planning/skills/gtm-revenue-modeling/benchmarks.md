# Revenue Modeling Benchmarks

Industry benchmarks and reference data for SaaS revenue modeling and GTM planning.

## Benchmark Disclaimer

**Important**: Benchmarks vary significantly by:
- Company stage (seed, growth, scale)
- Market segment (SMB, mid-market, enterprise)
- Industry vertical
- Geographic region
- Business model (PLG, sales-led, hybrid)

Use these as directional guidance, not absolute targets.

### T2D3 Framework

Standard hypergrowth trajectory:
- Year 1-2: Triple (3x)
- Year 3-4: Triple (3x)
- Year 5-6: Double (2x)
- Year 7+: Double (2x)

```
Year 1: $1M ARR
Year 2: $3M (3x)
Year 3: $9M (3x)
Year 4: $27M (3x)
Year 5: $54M (2x)
Year 6: $108M (2x)
```

### Magic Number

Sales efficiency metric: Net New ARR / Sales & Marketing Spend (prior Q)

| Rating | Magic Number | Interpretation |
|--------|--------------|----------------|
| Excellent | >1.0 | Highly efficient, invest more |
| Good | 0.75-1.0 | Efficient, growth-ready |
| Moderate | 0.5-0.75 | Average efficiency |
| Poor | <0.5 | Inefficient, optimize first |

## Rule of 40

Growth Rate % + Profit Margin % ≥ 40%

| Rating | Score | Assessment |
|--------|-------|------------|
| Elite | >60% | Top performer |
| Strong | 40-60% | Healthy balance |
| Moderate | 25-40% | Room for improvement |
| Weak | <25% | Needs attention |

### Trade-offs

| Growth | Profit | Total | Assessment |
|--------|--------|-------|------------|
| 60% | -10% | 50% | Growth mode, acceptable |
| 40% | 10% | 50% | Balanced, healthy |
| 20% | 25% | 45% | Efficient, moderate growth |
| 10% | 30% | 40% | Profitable, slow growth |

## Usage Guidelines

### Selecting Appropriate Benchmarks

1. **Company Stage**: Match to ARR level
2. **Segment Mix**: Weight by revenue contribution
3. **Industry**: Adjust for vertical specifics
4. **Geography**: Consider regional norms
5. **Business Model**: PLG vs sales-led varies

### Benchmark Sources

- **OpenView Partners**: SaaS benchmarks
- **KeyBanc**: Public SaaS metrics
- **Bessemer**: Cloud index
- **SaaS Capital**: Private company data
- **Paddle/ProfitWell**: Retention benchmarks

### Updating Benchmarks

- Review quarterly with industry reports
- Adjust for macro economic conditions
- Consider company-specific factors
- Document assumptions and sources
